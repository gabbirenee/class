Course Information

This course will focus on the students understanding of the design principles, the production process, and the tools (software and hardware) associated with development for the world wide web. The course builds on the foundations taught in CME106. Among the areas of emphasis will be graphics preparation, design principles, design theory, production processes, and active development of a web site using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. A variety of software applications will be used to develop projects.
